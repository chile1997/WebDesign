About


Chile Studio, found in February 2025 by Chile Nguyen, is a premier studio specializing fine art. As an artist focused on fine arts based in Missouri, Chile provides high-quality and original artwork to exhibitions and art connoisseurs who appreciate her unique artistic flair. While most of her work serves the professional world, she also pursues her projects. Chile explores her creativity through her art to express her perspectives on societal issues inspired by mythology, folklore, and natural landscapes. Chile Studio is also available for private commissions.

Meet the Artist




Chile Nguyen was known as a painter and printmaker. She had a strong love for painting and was a fan of Japanese manga. Her art was a mixture of European art and Japanese art and depicted her intense feelings for the great nature and the people around her. Chile uses her brushstroke technique to create detailed works that revealed her passion for beauty.

When she was young, she read the manga Inuyasha by Takahashi Rumiko. With the innocent eyes of a child, what attracted her at that time was not its content but the beautiful and attractive drawing. This is the comic that drew her to the world of art. She started creating countless paper dolls based on her favorite animal characters and telling her own stories to her parents.

She was born in 1997 and raised in Ho Chi Minh City in Vietnam. In Vietnam, she was not able to develop her art because the art of painting in Vietnam was not popular and it was only classified as entertainment. In 2012, her family immigrated to the United States. Her art has been deeply influenced by her professor Brain Smith at Saint Charles Community College in Missouri, Japanese comics, and also the classical drawings of Renaissance artists.
