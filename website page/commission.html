Process Behind
the Commission

Commissioning a bespoke work of art can seem like a daunting process, but we are here to guide you every step of the way. Our collaborative approach ensures that your vision comes to life beautifully. We have learned that the best results come from creative partnerships where the artist reflects the client’s personality as much as their appearance.

"A Discussion" by Louis Charles Moeller, Oil Painting, Private collection.

Stage One: Review Service Terms & Submit Your Request & Approve the Sketch

Before starting the commission process, we encourage you to carefully read our service terms.

Understanding the process in advance ensures smooth collaboration and helps avoid any uncertainties.

Submit Your Request

Once you're ready to proceed, please provide the following details:

  • Artwork size– Dimensions in inches
  • Theme & specific content– Portraits, landscapes, still life, or custom themes
  • Any special details or personalization requests– Specific colors, elements, ideas or inspirations

We will listen carefully to all your requests. The more details you provide, the more we can visualize and deeply understand your wishes, ensuring that the final artwork fully meets your expectations.

Quotation & Idea Confirmation

After reviewing your requirements, we will provide a preliminary quote based on the following factors:

  • Complexity of the artwork
  • Materials & techniques used
  • Estimated completion time

If you agree with the quoted price, we will proceed with creating a concept sketch.

Sketch Approval & Deposit Payment

A preliminary sketch will be sent to you for review & feedback. If necessary, minor revisions can be requested to refine the concept.

Once the sketch is approved, a 50% deposit must be paid based on the agreed terms before the painting process officially begins.

Stage Two: Creating Your Artwork

Once the sketch is approved and the deposit is paid, we start creating your artwork, following the agreed design.

Estimated Completion Time

Depending on the complexity of the artwork, this process may take several weeks or months.

Progress Updates & Feedback

We will provide regular progress updates, so you can follow the development of your artwork.

Minor adjustments can be suggested to ensure the final piece matches your expectations.

Our goal is to ensure that each piece of art is beautiful and reflects your vision and emotions!

Final Review & Completion

Once the artwork is completed, we will send high-resolution images for your final approval.

The remaining balance must be paid before shipping or pickup.

Important Note: At this step, no further revisions can be accepted.

Stage Three: Delivery & Finishing

Your commissioned artwork is now complete! We take extra steps to ensure that you are fully satisfied with the final piece before it reaches its new home.

Final Review & Approval: Before shipping, we will send high-resolution images for your final approval. Please note that no further revisions will be accepted at this stage.

Finishing Touches: We offer additional services, including professional framing, protective varnishing, and secure packaging to ensure the longevity and preservation of your artwork.

Turnaround Time: If your artwork is an oil painting, please allow approximately one month for the paint to dry completely before shipping.

Packaging & Shipping: Your artwork will be carefully wrapped in shockproof and impact-resistant packaging to guarantee safe transportation.

Shipping Method: We use insured shipping services, and you will receive a tracking number to monitor your shipment’s progress.

Shipping Cost: Fees may vary based on location. A detailed quote will be provided before shipping.

Certification & Documentation: If applicable, a certificate of authenticity will be included with your artwork.

Thank you for trusting us with your custom artwork! We hope it brings you joy and inspiration for years to come.
