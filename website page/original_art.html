Invest in My Work

I paint in a suggestive way, to create the impression of my subject striving to engage with the viewer. I encourage the eye to wander around the painting’s surface - being drawn by the atmosphere and light, to focus on a twist on what appears normal.

The image is accurate to the original, so I am confident that it will give you many years of pleasure if you choose to add it to your collection.

This is an original, it will not be copied. You are investing in a unique piece of work.

Thank you for taking the time to view my work. Please feel free to Contact me if you have any questions.
