About

Chile Studio, established in January 2025 by Chile Nguyen, is rooted in a passion for fine art. As an artist focused on fine arts based in Missouri, Chile provides high-quality and original artwork to exhibitions and art connoisseurs who appreciate her unique artistic flair. While most of her work serves the professional world, she also pursues her projects. Chile explores her creativity through her art to express her perspectives on societal issues inspired by mythology, folklore, and natural landscapes. Chile Studio is also available for private commissions. Do you have a concept? Feel free to reach out so we can bring your vision to life!
